Frequently asked questions

Things you might be wondering

Is it too late for me?
No. Each of us has our own rhythm. There is no perfect moment for change — only the moment you feel you no longer want to stay where you are. And that moment can be the beginning of a new chapter.
What actually happens in a session?
We talk. I ask questions, you hear yourself thinking out loud, and together we clarify what matters to you. I don't hand you ready-made solutions — I help you find your own, the ones you truly believe in.
How is coaching different from therapy or advice from a friend?
Therapy looks at past wounds, at healing. A friend tells you what she would do. Coaching starts from where you are now and looks forward: what you want, what's holding you back, and what step you can take. A space that offers safety, without pressure.
What happens in the free chemistry session?
It's a half-hour conversation where we meet. You tell me what's on your mind, I tell you how I work, and we see if we're a good fit. It's not a coaching session and there's nothing to prepare — just come as you are.
What if I feel it's not for me?
That's okay. That's what the chemistry session is for — so you can feel, before anything else, whether we fit. We set out on this road together only if you want to.
How do I pay?
The package is 1,500 lei and can be paid in two installments — half at the start, half midway. I'll walk you through all the steps at the chemistry session.
Do you coach in English?
Yes. Sessions can be held in Romanian or English, whichever feels more natural to you — online, wherever you are.
